Mim Remembers

June 8, 2016

Mom was selfless in everything she did, always thinking of others before herself. She knew what our favorite dishes were — whether it was dumplings or snow pea tips with shrimp — and would always make them when we came home and would send us back with bountiful goods, making sure our fridges were well-stocked.

She sacrificed her own comforts and convenience so that we could have more.

She encouraged us when we struggled and cheered for our accomplishments; she always had a way to push us toward our potential and wouldn’t let us give up.

She was strong-willed, loyal, caring and good to the core. Her generous spirit knew no bounds.

Mom, we love you dearly and will miss you forever.
